Harmony's Tunes is an animated television program targeting 2-to-7-year-old children that strives to improve their music literacy.

Animated Theme Song

When a young composer named Harmony has parts of her music deleted and uploaded to a faraway land by a pesky conductor, she must work with her musical superhero squad, The Tunes, to put the missing parts back into her music before it’s time for her performance!

Learn More With This Video

Harmony's Tunes aims to directly improve children’s music literacy by developing their ability to

read, write, listen to, and appreciate music,

and their understanding of

musical terms, instruments, and styles

while indirectly improving additional key areas including

math, reading, self expression, listening, processing, social competence, and problem solving

through the use of interactive and entertaining digital content.

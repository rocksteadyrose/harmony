Harmony's Tunes is an animated television program targeted at 4-to-8-year-old children that strives to improve their music literacy.

Learn More

Music literacy is the ability to read, create, listen to, understand, reflect, and appreciate music.

While music is an essential language that everyone can learn...

Over 1.3 million elementary school students do not have access to music instruction

Only 28% of early childhood centers employ a music specialist

Current music-based television programs do not focus on the numerous standards involved in becoming music literate and target preschoolers, thus limiting what they can teach

Harmony's Tunes plans to change this.

Harmony's Tunes aims to:

directly improve children’s music literacy by developing their ability to

read, write, listen to, and appreciate music,

and their understanding of

musical terms, instruments, and styles

while indirectly improving additional key areas including

math, reading, self expression, listening, processing, social competence, and problem solving

through the use of interactive and entertaining digital content.
